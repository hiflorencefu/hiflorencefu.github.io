Florence Fu (傅曦瑶) is a writer and designer based in the Bay Area, by way of New York. She holds a B.A. in Art History and a B.S. in Journalism from Northwestern University.

Currently, she’s an Editorial Assistant at Letterform Archive, where she writes about the collection, supports exhibitions projects, and shares her enthusiasm for letters with the public. She is excited about type design, media art, and polyglotism.
