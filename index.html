Florence Fu (傅曦瑶) is a writer and type designer in New York. She holds a B.A. in art history and a B.S. in journalism from Northwestern University, and is a graduate of Type West’s year-long type design certificate program.

Currently, she’s an intern at Sharp Type, a contributor to Letterform Archive, and researching the history & contemporary practice of graphic design and typography in East Asia.
