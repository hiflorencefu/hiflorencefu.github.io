Florence Fu 傅曦瑶 is a writer, researcher, and designer from New York, currently based in Chicago. She is pursuing an MA in Humanities at the University of Chicago, where she received a Japan Studies Fellowship from the Center for East Asian Studies. Her research focuses on 20th and 21st century visual and material culture from Japan, Taiwan, and South Korea.

She holds a B.A. in art history and a B.S. in journalism from Northwestern University, and is a graduate of Type West’s yearlong type design program. She is a contributor to Letterform Archive, Sharp Type Co. AIGA Eye on Design, and Communication Arts. Writing -- Mail -- Are.na
