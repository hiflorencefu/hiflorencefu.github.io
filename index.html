Florence Fu 傅曦瑶 is a writer, researcher, and designer from New York. She is currently based in Tokyo. Her research interests revolve around Japanese contemporary society, graphic design, typography, and visual culture.

From 2022 to 2023, she was a Toshizo Watanabe Fellow at the Inter-University Center for Japanese Language Studies, with additional support from the Ito Foundation. She holds a MA in Humanities from the University of Chicago, and a BA in Art History and a BS in Journalism from Northwestern University. In 2019, she completed Type West and designed Ginza. She has contributed to Letterform Archive, Sharp Type Co. AIGA Eye on Design, and Communication Arts.
