Florence Fu (傅曦瑶) is a writer and designer in New York. She holds a B.A. in Art History and a B.S. in Journalism from Northwestern University, and is a graduate of Type West’s year-long type design certificate program.

Currently, she’s an intern at Sharp Type and an Editorial Associate at Letterform Archive. Her research interests include visual and material culture in Japan, Taiwan, and Korea, specifically graphic design and typography during the colonial and contemporary periods.
