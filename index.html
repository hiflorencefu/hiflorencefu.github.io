Florence Fu (傅曦瑶) is a writer, designer, and emerging curator based in the Bay Area, by way of New York. She holds a B.A. in Art History and a B.S. in Journalism from Northwestern University.

Currently, she’s the Editorial Assistant at Letterform Archive, where she writes about the collection, supports exhibitions projects, and shares her enthusiasm for letters with the public. She is excited about east asian art, contemporary art, type design, and polyglotism.
