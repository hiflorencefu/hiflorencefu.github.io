Florence Fu 傅曦瑶 is a writer, researcher, and designer from New York. She is currently based in Tokyo and studying Japanese supported by the Toshizo Watanabe Fellows program. Her research focuses on 20th and 21st century visual and material culture from Japan and Taiwan.

She holds a MA in Humanities from the University of Chicago, and a BA in Art History and a BS in Journalism from Northwestern University. In 2019, she completed Type West and designed Ginza. She has contributed to Letterform Archive, Sharp Type Co. AIGA Eye on Design, and Communication Arts.
